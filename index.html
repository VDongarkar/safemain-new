About SafePath

At SafePath, we believe that no pothole should ever cost a life. Our mission is to bridge the communication gap between the people on the road and the authorities who maintain them. By turning every citizen into a pair of eyes for the city, we ensure that critical road damage is seen, categorized, and fixed—not in weeks, but in real-time.
